Our Story

CodeInBrain was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make quality coding education accessible to everyone. What started as a small community of passionate developers has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of students worldwide.

We believe that learning to code is not just about memorizing syntax, but about developing problem-solving skills and a creative mindset. Our project-based approach ensures that students gain practical experience while building their portfolio.
