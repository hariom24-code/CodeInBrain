Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply click on the "Enroll Now" button on the course page. You'll be directed to our secure payment gateway where you can complete your registration.

Do I get lifetime access to course materials?

Yes, once you enroll in a course, you get lifetime access to all course materials, updates, and the community forum.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Prerequisites vary by course. Beginner courses typically don't require any prior knowledge, while intermediate and advanced courses may require some basic understanding of programming concepts.

Do you offer any certifications?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you'll receive a CodeInBrain certificate that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile.
